Brownies

The tasiest brownies you will ever eat.

These brownies from AllRecipesare rich and fudgy and will melt in your mouth. They are also very easy to make.

You can eat them on their own or top with your choice of frosting to a little extra sweetness. Try it with this cream cheese frosting recipe: Cream Cheese Frosting

    Ingredients
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
    Steps
  1. Melt the butter on the stove.
  2. Remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.
  3. Beat in the dry ingredients.
  4. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
