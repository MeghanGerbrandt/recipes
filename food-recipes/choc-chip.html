Chocolate Chip Cookies

Tasty chewy chocolate chip cookies with crispy edges and chewy middles.

These chocolate chip cookies are easy to make but don't tast like it! They are soft and chewy with a cripsy outside.

These cookies are a popular recipe on AllRecipes and once you try them, you will taste why. They are a favorite for many.

    Ingredients
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
    Steps
  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Drop the dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
